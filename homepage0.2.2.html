Bio

Defne Inceoglu

Deaf-neh In-jeh-oh-lu

Is a multidisciplinary artist and writer currently living in Toronto, Canada. They thought they were going to be a musician, but gave it up to dedicate themselves to their studies. They hold a BA in The History of Art and Visual Culture. Currently, they are working towards an MA in Museum Studies at the University of Toronto. Defne ºs thesis work is focused on the representation of animal bodies in museum collections and gallery spaces through a post-humanist lens.

Occasionally, professionally, Defne has worked as a graphic designer and illustrator. This has been going on now for around eight years. Not as professionally, Defne likes to take photographs and paint pictures. In the past, professionally, Defne wrote music. Actually professionally, Defne works as a library assistant and an archival intern.

Defne is currently a contributing writer for the Digital Tattoo Project and Musings.
